MEDICARE HOSPITAL

Medicare is a 400+ bedded highly advanced healthcare facility offering services across all major medical disciplines including Oncology, Cardiology, Obstetrics, Gynaecology, Orthopaedics, Urology, Neurology, Minimal Access, Metabolic, Bariatric Surgery, and Kidney Transplants. In Medicare, experts have performed more than 30,000 operations including all advanced and basic surgeries. Furthermore, being one of the best hospitals in Delhi, it is equipped with the necessary infrastructure that is required to carry out such advanced surgeries. Emergency services are available 24*7 with a Blood Bank, ATM, and other amenities for proper patient care. Heart Failure Clinic, Spine & Backache Clinic, Pain Clinic, Headache Clinic, Diabesity Clinic, Advance Knee & Hip clinic are some speciality clinics in this hospital.
